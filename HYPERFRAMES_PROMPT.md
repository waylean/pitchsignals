# HyperFrames Prompt for PitchSignals Intro Video

Use this prompt in HyperFrames to create a short Chinese project-introduction video for **PitchSignals**.

```text
Create a 90-second HyperFrames video introducing "PitchSignals", an open-source football match prediction research tool.

Format:
- 16:9 landscape, 1920x1080.
- No voiceover required if not available, but every narration beat must appear as readable Chinese subtitles.
- Chinese primary text, with short English UI labels only where useful.
- Tone: lively World Cup opening, then professional research-lab product demo.
- Audience: people who do not usually watch football but want to understand matches more intelligently.
- Compliance tone: clearly frame the project as research, learning, and entertainment only. Do not present it as commercial advice or a certainty engine.

Visual style:
- Opening: World Cup atmosphere, night stadium, floodlights, green pitch, excited crowd silhouettes, match schedule energy.
- Product section: football-pitch green interface, data panels, fixture cards, probability bars, tactical field lines, calm research-lab feel.
- Palette: deep pitch green, bright lime accent, white text, muted cyan data highlights.
- Typography: bold Chinese title type for hooks, clean readable UI type for explanations and subtitles.
- Motion: energetic opening text, stadium-light reveal, smooth wipe transitions, data-panel reveals, fixture cards sliding in, probability bars filling.
- Avoid casino/cash/profit/transaction imagery. Avoid official tournament logos or implying affiliation.

Narrative structure:

Scene 1, lively cold open, 0-10s:
Visual: stadium lights turn on, green pitch lines sweep across the screen, schedule cards flash by.
On-screen title:
"四年一届的大赛又来啦！"

"大家是不是又开始熬夜看球了？"

"平时不看球，突然看世界杯"
"就会有点力不从心"

"只知道 C 罗、梅西、内马尔几个明星球员，真聊起来好像又不够用。"

On-screen text:
"想出去吹一下牛"
"伪装成嘉豪级懂哥？"

"那怎么办？可以试试让 AI 帮你一起看球。"

"PitchSignals"

"它不是告诉你一定会发生什么，而是帮你整理一场比赛背后的信息结构。"

"获取今明赛程"
"选择比赛"
"读取赛前市场数据"
"聚合公开信息"

"数据层 → 预测权重层 → 预测层 → 反馈优化层"

Timing:
- Total duration: 120-125 seconds.
- Keep the first 45 seconds energetic and conversational.
- After PitchSignals appears, transition into a calmer product-demo rhythm.
- Every scene must have a subtitle line near the bottom, large enough to read on mobile.

Animation constraints:
- Use transitions between every scene; no jump cuts.
- Every scene must have entrance animation.
- Keep subtitles inside safe margins.
- Do not overlap labels on top of screenshots; place labels in card headers or outside the screenshot area.
- Keep each text block balanced: avoid awkward single-character or single-word lines.
- Use clear football atmosphere without official tournament branding.
```
